Boundary Warning Response

LUMINA-30 incident review public reference material.

Purpose

This template is used when a case appears to approach a LUMINA-30 boundary concern but available facts are not yet complete.

Template

This case appears to raise a boundary concern under LUMINA-30 because it may involve AI-driven action, external impact, and uncertainty about effective human refusal.

The key issue is not whether AI was involved in general, but whether humans retained operationally effective final refusal authority before any irreversible impact occurred.

A boundary review should be conducted before the case is described as having meaningful human oversight.
