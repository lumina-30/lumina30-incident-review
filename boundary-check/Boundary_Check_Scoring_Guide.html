Boundary Check Scoring Guide

LUMINA-30 incident review public reference material.

Purpose

This guide provides a simple scoring aid for LUMINA-30 boundary checks.

Status

This is not a legal score, certification, or compliance rating.

It is a review aid for comparing evidence strength across cases.

Score Levels

0 — Not Applicable

The case does not involve AI-driven action, refusal authority, or irreversible impact.

1 — No Effective Refusal

Human refusal was absent, bypassed, unavailable, or operationally ineffective before irreversible impact.

2 — Not Verifiable

The review cannot verify whether human refusal was available and effective.

3 — Nominal Refusal Only

Formal human approval or oversight existed, but operational refusal effectiveness is weak or unclear.

4 — Effective Refusal Demonstrated

Records show that human refusal was available, operationally effective, and usable before irreversible impact.

Recommended Classification

ScoreClassification
0Not applicable
1Procedurally invalid for boundary-review purposes
2Not verifiable; treat as no effective refusal
3Weak refusal / nominal oversight
4Effective refusal demonstrated

Relationship to L30-CI

The 0–4 score is a review aid for comparing evidence strength.
It does not replace the final L30-CI condition label.
