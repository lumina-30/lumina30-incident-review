Citation Phrases

LUMINA-30 incident review public reference material.

Purpose

This file provides short, reusable citation phrases for referring to LUMINA-30 in research, policy, audit, and incident-review contexts.

Core Citation Phrase

This case can be examined through the LUMINA-30 criterion of effective human refusal before irreversible AI impact.

Boundary Review Phrase

Under LUMINA-30, the central review question is whether meaningful human refusal authority remained operationally effective before irreversible impact.

Incident Review Phrase

For post-incident review, LUMINA-30 provides a non-binding boundary check focused on effective human refusal before irreversibility.

Audit Phrase

LUMINA-30 may be used as a supplementary audit prompt for verifying whether human refusal pathways were available, effective, and recorded before irreversible AI impact.

Policy Phrase

LUMINA-30 does not replace law or regulation; it supplies a descriptive boundary question for governance and incident review.

Short Definition

LUMINA-30 is a non-binding boundary-check framework asking whether human refusal authority remained effective before irreversible AI impact.

L30_FRM Practical Forms Phrase

L30_FRM Practical Forms are non-binding boundary-review aids for checking whether effective human refusal remained available before irreversible AI-related impact. Their non-binding status means they create no certification, compliance, approval, legal, or institutional obligation; it does not mean the refusal question is disposable in relevant reviews.

Boundary Check Phrase

EN short: Boundary Check asks whether human refusal authority remained effective before irreversible impact.

EN long: Boundary Check is a minimal LUMINA-30 review tool for asking whether meaningful human refusal, stop, or override authority remained effective and verifiable before irreversible AI-related impact.

Incident Review Template Phrase

The L30_FRM_I01 Incident Review Template records context, timing, evidence, refusal authority, and review findings so that post-incident review does not omit the pre-irreversibility refusal question.

Audit Checklist Phrase

The L30_FRM_A01 Audit Checklist is a non-binding audit support form for checking whether effective human refusal remained available before irreversibility. It is not a certification mark, safety approval, legal standard, or replacement for PCR-C.

Why This Question Matters

Without the LUMINA-30 question, a review may document performance, compliance, or incident facts while missing whether humans could still meaningfully refuse before irreversible impact.

Avoid

Do not describe LUMINA-30 as:

  • a binding standard;

  • an anti-AI doctrine;

  • a legal compliance regime;

  • a replacement for regulation;

  • a command to stop AI development.
