Legal and Compliance Minimal Template

LUMINA-30 incident review public reference material.

Purpose

This file provides short text for legal, compliance, and accountability review contexts.

Minimal Text

LUMINA-30 does not provide a legal conclusion or compliance determination. It provides a descriptive boundary check: before irreversible AI impact occurred, was meaningful human refusal authority operationally effective and verifiable? If the refusal pathway is absent or unverifiable, the case should not be described as demonstrating effective human refusal.

Legal-Careful Phrases

Use:

  • boundary review

  • descriptive reference

  • non-binding

  • not a liability finding

  • not a compliance certification

Avoid

Avoid:

  • legal violation

  • certified safe

  • proven liable

  • binding standard

  • mandatory requirement
