Purpose

This note provides standards-adjacent terminology for explaining LUMINA-30 near AI standards, assurance, audit, and governance discussions. It does not claim that LUMINA-30 is an ISO, IEC, ITU, IEEE, national, sectoral, or institutional standard.

Status

This note is non-binding, descriptive, and unaffiliated with any external institution. It does not claim endorsement, adoption, recognition, approval, certification, compliance, legal effect, or institutional use by any external organization.

Attachment Text

LUMINA-30 should not be presented as a standard or compliance framework. It can be used as standards-adjacent terminology for a supplementary boundary question: whether formal human oversight remained operationally sufficient to preserve effective refusal before irreversible AI-related impact.
